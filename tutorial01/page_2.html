Here are some fun facts about dogs!

  • Dogs noses are wet to help absorb scent chemicals
  • Three dogs survived the Titanic sinking
  • The tallest dog in the world is 44 inches tall
  • 30% of Dalmatians are deaf in one ear
  • Dogs can genuinely get jealous when they see you display affection for another creature

Here is a little classification table for dogs!

Kingdom: Animalia
Class: Mammalia
Family: Canidae
Diet: Primarily carnivores
Life span: 10-13 years, on average
Number of breeds: 340
Largest Breed: Irish Wolfhound
Smallest breed: Chihuahua
